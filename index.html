About

I am a tattoo designer based in North West Rustenburg, I started my career in tattoo design 10 years ago when i discovered I have passion to tell stories using art. I believe every tattoo tells a story and has a different meaning to those that have chosen it. The industry of Tattoo design has grown bigger over the years and tattoo are no longer seen as taboo or unacceptable, that makes me inspired as an artist to give it my all when im working with clients from all over the world.

Mission and vision

The mission and vision that we have at our tattoo shop is to tell a story with each and every tattoo that we design. A tattoo design to us is very sacred and unique to the wearer that is why we make sure each and every client has a unique design that stands out and canoot be replicated. We use ink for pretty much all skin colors for most races, our inks are to quality that will make your skin thank you and last longer for years without any signs of damage that may require redesigning over the years.
